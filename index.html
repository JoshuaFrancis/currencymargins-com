Platform overview

Three products that define a serious margin trading operation.

01

Perpetual futures

Cross-margined and isolated-margin perpetual futures contracts on BTC, ETH, SOL, and the top 50 assets by open interest, with leverage up to 100x and a funding rate mechanism that anchors contract prices to spot. An on-chain proof-of-reserves system publishes exchange wallet balances hourly, and a three-tier liquidation engine with an insurance fund backstop protects solvent positions from auto-deleveraging cascades.

02

Options and structured products

Vanilla and exotic options on major crypto assets with European-style settlement, a built-in strategy builder for spreads, straddles, and iron condors, and a visual payoff diagram that shows maximum profit, maximum loss, and breakeven at expiry. Institutional structured product issuance tools allow market makers to create and distribute principal-protected notes and yield enhancement products to eligible retail clients.

03

Collateralised lending

A borrow-and-lend market where holders post BTC, ETH, or stablecoin collateral to borrow against their positions without triggering a taxable disposition. Dynamic interest rates set by supply and demand, real-time loan-to-value monitoring with margin call alerts at configurable thresholds, and partial repayment tools that let borrowers reduce liquidation risk without fully closing their position.
